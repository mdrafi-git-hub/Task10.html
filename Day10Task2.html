Squid Game Web Series

Season - 1

5.6 billion cash prize. The games seem like childhood activities, but soon turn into deadly battles for survival. Seong Gi-hun, the main protagonist, struggles through betrayals, alliances, and moral dilemmas. Sang-woo’s ruthless ambition and Kang Sae-byeok’s tragic past add emotional depth to the story. In the end, Gi-hun wins but refuses to spend the money, and the shocking twist reveals Oh Il-nam as the game’s creator.

Season - 2

Season 2 will follow Gi-hun’s revenge mission after he chooses to stay and uncover the truth behind the games.
